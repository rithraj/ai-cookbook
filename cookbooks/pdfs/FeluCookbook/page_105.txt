 with some protein. In this case, this is 
 the perfect recipe for these moments.

D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Chop all vegetables into bite-sized pieces.  
Cut the chicken into strips.
2.	 Add all sauce ingredients into a small glass  
and mix until smooth.
3.	 Place a bowl on a scale.  
Add all vegetables except the onion.
4.	 Add 1 spray of oil to a pan  
(24cm/9,5inch) on medium-high. 
5.	 Add chicken strips and fry on one side  
for 1 minute until they get color. 
6.	 Add onions to the pan and flip the chicken.  
Make sure the onions touch the pan.  
Keep frying for another 90 seconds until cooked 
through.
7.	 Add the chicken and onions to bowl.  
Drizzle with the sauce. 
8.	 Mix everything. Done!
N O T E S :
•	Feel free to switch vegetables.  
Red bell pepper, carrots, and avocado work  
really well with this recipe.
