LIME 
CHEESECAKE 
W A T C H  V I D E O
 Another cheesecake variation  
 but this time with lime and no 
 protein powder. 
I N G R E D I E N T S  F O R  4  S E R V I N G S : 
340 g 	 	
0.2 % fat cream cheese 
340 g 	 	
skyr or 0.2 % fat greek yogurt 
2	
	
egg
30 g 	
	
all purpose flour 
50 g 	
	
sweetener
4	
	
lime zest
1/2 	
	
lime juice
ESTIMATED TIME: 50 MIN
D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Pre heat the oven to 160°C or 320°F.
2.	 Place a bowl on a scale, add all the ingredients, and 	
	
whisk until smooth.
3.	 Line a 12 × 18 cm (5 × 7 inch) baking dish with 	 	
	
parchment paper.
4.	 Transfer the mixture into the baking dish and bake 	
	
for 45–50 minutes.
5.	 Once the cake is done, remove it from the oven and 	
	
let it cool for about 15 minutes. Then refrigerate it for 	
	
at least 4 hours, or overnight for best results.
N O T E S :
•	
Top with 10 g of whipped cream right before serving.
•	
You can also use a butter cookie when you want a 	
	
slice. It’s more flexible and tastes just as good.
