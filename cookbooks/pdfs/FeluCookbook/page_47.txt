W A T C H  V I D E O
 Ever tried sweet tortillas for  
 breakfast? These taste amazing and  
 so easy to prepare in the morning. 
ESTIMATED TIME: 5 MIN
PB & BANANA	 
TORTILLA
I N G R E D I E N T S  F O R  1  S E R V I N G :
1 	
	
wheat tortilla (20cm or 8inch)
10 g 	
	
peanut butter
1	
	
medium banana or 125 g
100 g 	 	
skyr or 0.2% fat greek yogurt

D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Peel and slice up the banana.
2.	 Heat up a pan and dry fry the tortilla for  
1 minute on each side until it has brown spots.
3.	 Remove the tortilla from the pan and place  
it on a plate. 
4.	 Spread peanut butter into the middle of the  
tortilla. Add greek yogurt and  
then the sliced banana.
5.	 Roll up the tortilla into a burrito shape. Done!
N O T E S :
•	You can top the tortilla with 10 g of honey for  
some added sweetness.
