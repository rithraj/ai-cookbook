D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Dice the onion. Peel and grate garlic.
2.	 Add water with broth powder to a glass and mix 	
	
	
until combined.
3.	 Heat a pan (24cm/9,5inch) to medium and add in 	
	
	
oil.
4.	 Add the onions and fry them for 3 minutes until they 	 	
	
caramelize.
5.	 Add in another bit of oil and add in garlic and fry them 		
	
for 20 seconds.
6.	 Mix everything and add the broth and tomato sauce.
7.	 Next add in a strong pinch of salt, black pepper and 	
	
	
oregano.
8.	 Let it simmer for 5 minutes.
9.	 Add in laughing cow and mix it into the sauce by pres	 	
	
sing it down until the tomato sauce turns orange - this 		
	
takes about 1 minute.
10.	Add in optional basil at the very end before serving, 	
	
	
done!
N O T E S :
•	This tastes great over chicken and rice.
•	Ad in chili flakes for some kick.
