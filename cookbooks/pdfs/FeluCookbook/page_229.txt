D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 In a small bowl, combine the turmeric and cumin. 		
	
In another bowl, add 120 g of water and the stock 	
	
powder, then stir until combined.
2.	 Wash the rice in cold water, drain it, and repeat 	 	
	
once more.
3.	 Heat a pan (18 cm/7 inch) over medium heat, add 		
	
the oil, then add the spices and toast them for 	
	
	
about 1 minute until fragrant.
4.	 Add the washed rice and toast it for another 	
	
	
minute, adding a pinch of salt.
5.	 Pour in the broth, reduce the heat to low, stir once, 	
	
then cover the pan with a lid. Let it simmer for 12 		
	
minutes.
N O T E S :
•	You can add a peeled piece of ginger during the 	
	
	 simmering step to enhance the fresh flavor.
