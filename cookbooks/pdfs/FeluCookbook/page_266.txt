D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Place a bowl on a scale.
2.	 Peel and grate the garlic and add it to the bowl.
3.	 Add all other ingredients,  
and squeeze in the juice of the lemon.
4.	 Mix until smooth. Done!
N O T E S :
•	The more traditional tzatziki is made with white 
vinegar instead  lemon juice and, finely sliced cu-
cumber that needs to be squeezed in a paper towel 
to remove any liquid before being added to the 
tzatziki.
•	It can be stored in the fridge covered for 3-5 days.
