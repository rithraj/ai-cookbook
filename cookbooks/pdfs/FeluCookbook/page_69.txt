D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Start by adding rice and 125g of water to a pot  
	
with a pinch of salt.
2.	 Put the stove on high heat, and as soon as the water 
starts to boil, turn the heat down to low,  
close the lid and let it simmer for 12-15 min.
3.	 Cut the chicken thighs into large pieces and 	 	
	
	
season with salt, black pepper, and garlic powder
4.	 Chop the onion and bell pepper. Peel and grate the 	
	
	
garlic and ginger.
5.	 Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat and fry the 	 	
	
chicken for 1 minute on each side to add some color.
6.	 Add the onion and bell pepper, frying for 3 minutes 
until the onion starts to caramelize.
7.	 Add the garlic and ginger, mixing everything well. 	
	
	
Continue to fry for another 20 seconds.
8.	 Now, add the sauce and stir for 30 seconds until it thi-
ckens. To serve, plate the rice and top it with the sweet 
& sour chicken. Enjoy!
N O T E S :
•	Add gochujang to the sauce for some heat.
•	You can add 50g of canned pineapple to the finished dish. 	
	 That tastes great here.
