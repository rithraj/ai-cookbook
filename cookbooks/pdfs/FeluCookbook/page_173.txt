PB & STRAWBERRY 
OATMEAL
I N G R E D I E N T S  F O R  
1  S E R V I N G : 
50 g 	
	
oats 
150 g 	 	
strawberries frozen 
13 g 	
	
powdered peanuts 
30 g 	
	
whey vanilla protein powder
250 g	 	
milk
Salt
D I R E C T I O N S :
1. Start by placing a bowl on a scale and add the 
oats. Toast the oats for 2 minutes on medium 
heat until they become slightly brown.
2. Add milk, strawberries, and a pinch of salt. Keep 
stirring occasionally until the oatmeal thickens, 
which should take about 3 minutes.
3. Remove the oats from the heat and transfer 
them to a bowl. Then, add whey protein powder 
and powdered peanuts and mix well. Done!
N O T E S :
•	Top with walnuts and/or banana for additional 	
	 flavour but please track their calories.
•	Powdered peanuts can be replaced with  
10 g of regular peanut butter.
 This speedy oatmeal is the perfect 
 start to your day!
