1  S E R V I N G : 
350 g 	 	
milk 1.5% 
45 g 	
	
whey vanilla protein powder
Salt
50 g	
	
liquid coffee
NINJA CREAMI IS NEED FOR THE RECIPE. 
ALTERNATIVELY BUY A LOW CALORIE VA-
NILLA ICE CREAM LIKE HALO TOP!
D I R E C T I O N S :
1. Start by placing the pint on a scale.
2. Add milk, protein powder, and a pinch of salt. 
Close the lid and give it a shake. Put in the free-
zer for at least 24 hours.
3. Place the pint in a Ninja creami and press the Ice 
Cream Button. Repeat one more time.
4. Make fresh coffee, espresso or regular coffee.
5. Scoop out ice cream and place in a bowl and 
pour coffee over the ice cream, done!
 An absolute classic italian dessert!
