 Creamy mashed potatoes are  
 great as a side, especially with  
 lean, grilled meat!

I N G R E D I E N T S  F O R  1  S E R V I N G :
250 g 	 	
potatoes 
50 g 	
	
skimmed milk
1 	
	
laughing cow wedge 
Salt and black pepper
D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Clean the potatoes and cut them into smaller pie-
ces.
2.	 Put them into a pot of salted water and let them 	 	
	
simmer on medium heat for 12-15 minutes.
3.	 After the potatoes are soft, drain the water and 
add them to a bowl. Mash them with a fork until 
they turn to a smooth consistency.
4.	 Then, add milk and mix until smooth. Add 		
	
	
Laughing Cow cheese and the seasoning of your 		
	
choice, and continue mixing until the cheese 	
	
	
is fully incorporated. Done!
N O T E S :
•	Try adding fresh herbs like rosemary and seasoning 	
	 such as cayenne pepper or cumin for extra flavor.
