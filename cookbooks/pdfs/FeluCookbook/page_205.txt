ESTIMATED TIME: 10 MIN
BIG BOYS 
PANCAKES
D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Grind the oats in a food processor or blender for 
around 60 seconds or until it becomes oat flour.
2.	 Preheat a pan to medium heat.
3.	 Place a bowl on your scale. 
4.	 Add all dry ingredients together and mix well.
5.	 Place another bowl on the scales and add  
all wet ingredients and mix well.
6.	 Add the wet to the dry ingredients and  
mix until smooth.
7.	 Add oil to a pan on medium heat.
8.	 Add the pancake batter and wait until it starts  
to form delicate bubbles on top of the batter.  
This should happen after 2-3 minutes.
9.	 Flip and cook again for 2-3 minutes.
10.	Remove and repeat with the entire batter.  
Done!
N O T E S :
•	Pancakes go really well with Greek yogurt  
as a topping.
