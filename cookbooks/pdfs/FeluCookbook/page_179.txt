W A T C H  V I D E O
 Banana bread is a classic childhood   
 favorite but apple bread is equaly if  
 not more delicious!

ESTIMATED TIME: 30 MIN
D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Start by grinding the oats up in a blender for  
60 seconds until it becomes oat flour.
2.	 Place a bowl on a scale.
3.	 Add all dry ingredients.  
Whisk everything until combined.
4.	 Add apples with cottage cheese into a blender  
and mix until smooth, around 30 secs.  
Add mixture into a separate bowl.
5.	 Add egg and egg whites into the bowl with apples and mix.
6.	 Transfer wet to dry ingredients.
7.	 Whisk again until creamy and smooth.
8.	 Place parchment paper into a baking sheet  
(8x22cm or 3x9 inch).
9.	 Transfer batter into baking sheet and bake for  
20-25 min at 180°C or 350°F.
10.	After baking remove cake from the baking sheet  
and let it cool down for 30 min.
11.	 Cut and enjoy.  
The apple bread lasts in the fridge for 2-3 days. 
N O T E S :
•	
You can add 1-2 tablespoons of cinnamon for extra flavor.
•	
Apple bread is great with some whipped cream or/and 
vanilla ice cream.
•	
Great breakfast topped with greek yogurt.
•	
Whey protein is recommended here because  
casein doesn’t rise properly when baked.
