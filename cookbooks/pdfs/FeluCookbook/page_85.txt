D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Chop up the onion and cut the chicken into strips.
2.	 Add all sauce ingredients into a small bowl  
and mix until smooth.
3.	 Fill up a pot with water and turn  
on the heat on high.
4.	 Add oil to a pan (24cm/9,5inch) and fry the chicken 
on one side for 60 seconds on medium-high.
5.	 After a minute, add onion to the pan and flip  
the chicken. Add a pinch of salt and pepper.  
Add udon noodles to the pot that should be  
boiling by now.
6.	 Add the sauce to the pan with chicken and  
stir on medium-low heat until the Udon noodles  
are ready. 2-3 minutes.
7.	 Transfer the Udon noodles to a pan and mix  
everything. Done!
N O T E S :
•	The sauce can be changed as long as you stick with 
East Asian style ingredients. Try to experiment with 
hoisin or gochujang.
