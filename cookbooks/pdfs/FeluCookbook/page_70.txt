D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Add 125 g of water to a pot (18cm or 7inch diameter)  
with the rice and a pinch  salt.
2.	 Put the pot on the stove on high heat.  
As soon the water starts to boil, reduce the heat to low, 
close the lid and let it simmer for 12-15 minutes.
3.	 Cut the chicken breast into strips. Dice the onion,  
and add all sauce ingredients into a glass and combine.
4.	 Add the chicken and onions to a 18cm or 7-inch pan. 
Add in the sauce as well.
5.	 Put the pan on a stove on medium and let it simmer  
for 5-8 minutes, depending on your chicken size. 
6.	 Crack 2 eggs into a small bowl and scramble them.  
Add salt and pepper.
7.	 As soon the chicken is almost cooked through, add in 
the eggs without mixing. Close the lid and let it simmer 
on medium-low heat for 3-5 minutes. 
8.	 Add the rice to a bowl, and top with the chicken  
egg mixture. Done!
N O T E S :
•	If you use low-sodium soy you might need to  
add more salt at the end.
