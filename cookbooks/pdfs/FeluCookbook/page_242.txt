 A super speedy sandwich made 
 out of rice cakes.

I N G R E D I E N T S  F O R  1  S E R V I N G :
2	
	
rice cakes
30 g	
	
deli meat
15 g	
	
cream cheese 
15 g	
	
tomatoes
15 g	
	
lettuce
Salt and black pepper
D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Place one rice cake on a plate. 
2.	Add cream cheese, then deli meat, 	
	
	
followed by lettuce and tomatoes, and 	
	
	
lastly, sprinkle with salt and black pepper.	
3.	Top with the other rice cake, and you’re 	 	
	
done!
N O T E S :
•	Try different kinds of deli meat as long as 	 	
	 they are lean.
•	You can add some sriracha or any other 	
	
	 low-calorie sauce for a kick.
