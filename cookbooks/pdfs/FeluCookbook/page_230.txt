 Naan bread is a very popular recipe, 
 and this version is as good as an  
 authentic recipe, but also can be made 
 in 15 minutes!

2 INGREDIENT NAAN
BREAD DOUGH
I N G R E D I E N T S  F O R  1  S E R V I N G :
50 g 	
	
all purpose flour
60 g 	
	
skyr or 0.2 % fat greek yogurt
½ tsp 	 	
baking powder or 3 g
1
  g	
	
garlic powder
1
  g	
	
onion powder
2 g 	
	
oil
Salt and black pepper
D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Place a bowl on a scale.
2.	 Add all ingredients and combine with your hands 
for around 60 seconds. It will slowly turn into a 
dough ball.
3.	 Let it rest in the bowl for 15 minutes.
4.	 Add oil into a nonstick pan (24cm/9,5inch).  
Don’t switch the heat on the stove yet.  
Add in the dough ball.
5.	 Press the ball down and press the edges  
slowly until it turns to a flat round bread.  
This dough will fill 24cm or 9.5 inches.
6.	 Switch the heat to medium and cook on both  
sides for 3-4 minutes with occasional flipping  
until both sides have a golden brown color.
N O T E S :
•	The dough can be also turned into a pizza shape on 
a baking sheet and baked in the oven at 200°C for 
10 minutes with pizza toppings for speedy pizza.
