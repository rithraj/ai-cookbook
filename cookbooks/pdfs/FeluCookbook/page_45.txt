D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Cut the strawberries into bite-size pieces.
2.	 Place a bowl on a scale and add yogurt, then  
berries and honey on top. Mix everything together.
3.	 Chop up the walnuts or break them with your 
hands and sprinkle on top of your yogurt bowl.
