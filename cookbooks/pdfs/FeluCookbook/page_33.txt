D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Dice the onion, and peel and grate the garlic.
2.	 Weigh out the ingredients using a scale and add the 
tomato puree, soy sauce, gochujang, water and honey  
to a bowl. Mix until smooth.
3.	 Add the oil to a pan on medium heat. Start by frying  
the onions for roughly 3 minutes until they caramelize. 
4.	 Add in the garlic and keep frying for a further  
20 seconds.
5.	 Add the sauce to the pan and mix everything well.  
Allow to simmer for 30 seconds. Then crack in  
3 eggs in 3 different spots.
6.	 Close the lid and allow it cook for 3-5 minutes or  
until the eggs are done but not overcooked.  
You’re ready to serve!
N O T E S :
•	This Korean style breakfast tastes amazing and with  
2 slices of toasted bread it’s incredible.
•	Top with some fresh cilantro or parsley.
•	Dice half a red bell pepper and fry it in the beginning  
with the onion for added volume and flavor.
