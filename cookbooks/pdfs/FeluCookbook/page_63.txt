D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Heat up a cast iron pan on medium-high.  
Add oil to the pan.
2.	 Add a tortilla and let it cook for 2 minutes  
until the bottom gets color.
3.	 Spread the tomato sauce, deli meat and  
Mozzarella on top. 
4.	 Season with salt, pepper, and oregano.
5.	 Put the cast iron pan under the broiler on  
top  a baking sheet for 2-3 minutes until  
the cheese starts to become brown and bubbly.
6.	 Remove the homemade pizza and enjoy!
