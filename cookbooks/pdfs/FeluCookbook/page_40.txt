 snack during the day or they can be  
 used as an easy breakfast when made    
 in a big batch!

D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Preheat the oven to 180°C or 350°F.
2.	 Chop up the tomato and spinach into  
very small pieces.
3.	 Add all the ingredients into a bowl and  
mix everything until combined.
4.	 Distribute the eggs into 6 muffin cases  
(Diameter 7cm or 3inch)
5.	 Bake the egg white bites for 15-18 minutes.
6.	 Remove them from the muffin tin and  
immediately enjoy or store in the fridge.
N O T E S :
•	You can get creative with the spices for different 
flavor combinations. Paprika, smoked paprika,  
turmeric, and cayenne pepper all work great.
•	Change the cheese to 25 g of shredded cheddar  
for a taste twist. This recipe will have 30 more  
calories with cheddar.
