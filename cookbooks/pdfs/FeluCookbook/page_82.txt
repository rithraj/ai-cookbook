I N G R E D I E N T S  F O R  1  S E R V I N G :
1 	
	
chicken breast or 180 g

1

6 g

1 g

Salt and black pepper
S A U C E :
20 g 	
	
peaunut butter
20 g 	
	
soy sauce
15 g 	
	
honey
1/2	
	
lime
D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Add all sauce ingredients to a small bowl or glass and 	 	
	
mix until combined.
2.	 Slice the chicken breast into thin slices. Then chop the 		
	
vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
3.	 Add 2 g of oil to a 24 cm (9.5 inch) pan over medium-
high heat and stir-fry the vegetables with a pinch of salt 
for 3–4 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
4.	 Meanwhile, fill a pot with water and set it on the stove 
over medium-high heat. Do not add the noodles yet.
5.	 PPush the vegetables to the side of the pan, add 	
	
	
another 2 g of oil, and then add the chicken breast. 	
	
	
Season with salt, black pepper, and garlic powder. 	
	
	
Stir-fry the chicken for 1 minute on each side.
6.	 Add the udon noodles, along with a pinch of salt, to the 
boiling water. They will need about 3 minutes to cook.
7.	 Remove the chicken and vegetables from the pan. 
Return the empty pan to the stove, add the sauce along 
with a splash of the noodle water, and let it simmer for 1 
minute.
8.	 Add the cooked noodles to the pan and stir. Finally, 	
	
	
return the chicken and vegetables to the pan and give 		
	
everything a good mix. Done!
