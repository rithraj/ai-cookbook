D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Start by grinding the oats in a food processor  
or blender for around 60 seconds or until it  
becomes oat flour.
2.	 Next thoroughly rinse the kidney beans  
to remove the liquid. 
3.	 Add the kidney beans and all other ingredients 
to the food processor.
4.	 Blend until smooth in consistency.  
Add a small splash of water if it sticks.
5.	 Place parchment paper onto a baking tray 
(20x20cm or 8x8inch).
6.	 Transfer the batter to the baking tray and  
bake for around 25-30 min at 180°C or 350°F.
7.	 Let the brownies cool down for 15 min.
8.	 Cut the brownies into equal squares and enjoy. 
They last in the fridge for 2-3 days.
N O T E S :
•	Brownies taste best when warm from the oven 
and topped with Greek yogurt or low-calorie 
vanilla ice cream.
