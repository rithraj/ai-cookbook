D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Add rice and water to a big pot with a pinch of salt. Place 
it on high heat on the stove. Once the water starts to 
boil, reduce the heat to low, close the lid, and let it sim-
mer for 12-15 minutes.
2.	 Preheat the oven to 200°C or 400°F.
3.	 In a big bowl, add chicken thighs, 4g oil, 6g garlic 	
	
	
powder, 6g smoked paprika, 3g cayenne pepper, 	
	
	
3g cumin, black pepper, and a big pinch of salt. Mix 	
	
	
everything until all thighs are covered with seasoning.
4.	 Transfer everything into a baking sheet, separate the 	 	
	
thighs, and bake them for 15 minutes.
5.	 Chop tomatoes, onions, bell pepper, and jalapenos into 	
	
bite-size pieces and add them to a bowl. Add a bunch 		
	
of chopped cilantro, a pinch of salt, black pepper, and 	 	
	
the juice of 1 lime. Mix everything.
6.	 Cut the avocado in half and add the flesh to a bowl, 	
	
	
followed by cream cheese, a pinch of salt, black pepper, 	
	
3g of garlic powder, and the juice of 1 lime. Mix 	
	
	
everything with a fork until it turns to mush.
7.	 Once the chicken thighs are ready, lay them on a 	
	
	
cutting board and cut them into bite-size pieces. 	
	
	
Prepare 5 meal prep containers and distribute the 	
	
	
rice, the avocado spread, the pico de gallo, and the 	
	
	
chicken evenly. Done!
N O T E S :
•	The avo spread will not turn brown if you have meal 	
	 prep container that are air tight!
