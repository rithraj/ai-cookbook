D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Place a bowl on your scale.
2.	 Add all ingredients to the bowl.  
If you can’t find shredded carrots,  
grate 1 carrot with a box grater.
3.	 Mix everything until smooth.
4.	 Refrigerate overnight. 
5.	 You’re done!
