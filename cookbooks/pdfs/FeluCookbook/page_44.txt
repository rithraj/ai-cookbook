D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Place a bowl on a scale. Add the oat flour, sweetener, 	 	
	
baking powder, and baking soda, and mix. 
2.	 Melt the butter (in a microwave or in a pot on the stove) 	
	
for about 30 seconds. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
3.	 In a separate bowl, combine the skyr, egg whites, melted 
butter, vanilla extract, orange zest, and blueberries. Mix 
well.
4.	 Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and stir 
until smooth.
5.	 Line a baking dish (18 × 12 cm or 7 × 5 inches) with 	
	
	
parchment paper, then transfer the batter. Bake for 40 		
	
minutes.
6.	 Let the cake cool for about 30 minutes before serving. 	 	
	
Done!
N O T E S :

	 then use this mixture as a frosting for the cake.
