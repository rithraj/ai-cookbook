 A famous german dessert that you can 
 now enjoy every day. 
ESTIMATED TIME: 10 MIN
BANANA SPLIT
D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Place a bowl on a scale and add the pudding 	
	
	
powder and milk. Using an electric hand 	 	
	
	
mixer, mix until it reaches a pudding-	
	
	
	
like consistency (about 60 seconds).
2.	 Add the casein to the bowl and continue mixing 	 	
	
until thick.
3.	 Slice a banana, then layer with Greek yogurt, 1/3 	 	
	
of the custard, and blueberries.
4.	 Store the remaining custard in the fridge for up 	 	
	
to one week.
N O T E S :
•	Feel free to use different flavors of pudding or 	
	
	 protein powder to vary the taste.
