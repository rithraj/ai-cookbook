D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Add salt, black pepper, oregano, and garlic powder 	
	
on both sides of the chicken, and then air fry them 	
	
at 200°C or 400°F for 12 minutes.
2.	 Add all sauce ingredients into a glass and mix until 	
	
smooth.
3.	 Chop up all the vegetables, feta, and avocado into 	
	
bite-size pieces.
4.	 Prepare a massive bowl. Add the sauce and 	
	
	
vegetables, and mix everything. Now, add the 	
	
	
avocado and cheese and mix again. Lastly, top the 	
	
salad with the air-fried chicken. Done!
