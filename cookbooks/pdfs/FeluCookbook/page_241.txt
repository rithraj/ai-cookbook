I N G R E D I E N T S  F O R  1  S E R V I N G :

bunch	 	
cilantro or 10 g
Salt and black pepper
D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Cut all the vegetables into bite-sized 	
	
	
pieces and add them to a large bowl.
2.	Squeeze the juice of one lime over the 	
	
	
vegetables, add a pinch of salt and black 		
	
pepper, and mix everything together. 
3.	Top the salad with a bunch of chopped 	 	
	
cilantro. Done!
N O T E S :
•	This salad makes a great side dish for a 	
	
	 grilled chicken breast.
•	You can also add some spices like cumin or 	
	 garlic powder.
