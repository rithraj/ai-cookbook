 Caesar chicken in a wrap tastes 
 absolutely delicious! 
ESTIMATED TIME: 50 MIN
CAESAR CHICKEN
WRAPS
I N G R E D I E N T S  F O R  4  S E R V I N G S :
4 	
	
wheat tortillas (25 cm or 10 inch)
600 g 	 	
chicken breast
200 g	
	
deli meat
15 g 	
	
olive oil
4 g 	
	
garlic powder
4 g 	
	
cayenne pepper
Salt and black pepper
40 g	
	
parmesan cheese
2	
	
romaine lettuces
40 g	
	
light mayo
20 g	
	
mustard
1/2	
	
lemon
1	
	
clove of garlic
100 g 	 	
skyr or 0.2 % fat greek yogurt
Salt
D I R E C T I O N S :
1.	 Place a bowl on a scale and add all the sauce ingredients; mix well.
2.	 Chop the romaine lettuce and grate the Parmesan cheese, then 	
	
add them to the bowl and mix.
3.	 Slice the chicken into bite-sized pieces and place them in a 	
	
	
separate bowl. Add olive oil, garlic powder, cayenne pepper, salt, 	
	
and black pepper, then mix everything thoroughly.
4.	 Cut the deli meat into small pieces and set aside.
5.	 Heat a little oil in a pan over medium heat, then add the chicken 	
	
and fry for 1 minute on each side.
6.	 Add the deli meat, stir, and continue frying for 30 more seconds. 	
	
Remove from the heat. 
7.	 Prepare the wraps and lay them out on a table. Distribute the 	 	
	
filling evenly among all four wraps and roll them tightly 	
	
	
into burrito shapes.
8.	 Wrap them in parchment paper and store in the fridge for up to 	
	
5 days. 
N O T E S :
•	The wraps taste amazing cold from the firdge.
•	You can also microwave them for 2-3 minutes.
